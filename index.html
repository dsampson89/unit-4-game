Crystal Collector

A Game of Fate

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal below. By clickin on the crysals you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden to you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
